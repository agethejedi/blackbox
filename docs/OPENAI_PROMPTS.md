# Prompt Design

## Neutrality Rules
- Do not decide who is right.
- Do not diagnose people.
- Do not provide legal or therapy advice.
- Identify observable communication patterns and coaching opportunities.

## Analysis Outputs
- Quality score
- Four Horsemen
- Repair Index
- Topic Drift
- Loops
- Conflict outcome
- Unanswered questions
- Coaching recommendations

## Coach Mode
Focus on:
- Validation
- Brevity
- Scope control
- De-escalation
- Next best response
